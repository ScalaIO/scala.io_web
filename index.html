The Scala and FPL friendly event in Paris !

October 24th and 25th, 2013. Paris, France

For Scala and functional programing

Scala.io is the event of the year in Paris about the Scala Programming language and Functional programing as a whole.

It will tackle not only Scala/JVM growing ecosystem but also functional programming languages like Haskell, Clojure, Erlang...

The venue is going to be awesome and Top Speakers and influencers of the Scala-tech world are already on their way.

Venue

The venue will be hosted in a really nice place in the 17th arrondissement of Paris.

Speakers

The call for proposal will open Monday July 7th and close Sunday August 25th.
